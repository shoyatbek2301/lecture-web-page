Star Map Game Design Plan

Gameplay Flow

  • Player views the star map with all systems and warp path costs
  • Player taps systems in the order they wish to visit them
  • A route line appears showing the planned journey
  • Energy cost updates as each system is added to the route
  • Player can rearrange route by dragging systems in the sequence list
  • When satisfied, player executes the route and watches the ship travel
  • Final score and rating appear upon completion

Step 5: Test - Feedback Collection Points

  • Is the objective clear immediately?
  • Do players understand how to plan routes?
  • Is the scoring system intuitive?
  • Do players feel satisfied when completing efficient routes?
  • Are the visual cues helpful for decision-making?
  • What additional features would enhance gameplay?

Implementation Plan

Basic Prototype (Phase 1)

  • Static star map with 6 systems
  • Click-to-select route planning
  • Basic energy calculation
  • Simple success/fail state

Enhanced Version (Phase 2)

  • Multiple difficulty levels with different map sizes
  • Animated ship movement
  • Star ratings for efficiency
  • Custom themes (space, fantasy, etc.)

Full Release (Phase 3)

  • Campaign mode with progressive challenges
  • Special mission objectives
  • Leaderboards
  • Route sharing functionality
